About Me

Hi guys! Thank you for visiting my blog! My name is Isa, and I am a sophomore at Tufts University near Boston, MA, but I’m originally from North Carolina. I am currently studying Human Factors Engineering and Computer Science, which means I am super interested in how humans experience the online world. Fun fact: I coded this website myself! A little bit more about me: I love thrifting, tap dancing, dogs, and I am seriously addicted to bubble tea.

The Story of Fashionisa

Throughout my life I’ve used fashion as a creative outlet to express myself, whether it be dressing up to go out of the house, or sewing and designing clothes. During quarantine 2020, I realized I had lost this creative outlet since I had to stay home in my pajamas every day. Shortly after this realization, I started thinking of ways I could still express myself and perhaps inspire others while doing so. Thus, my blog was born. My main goal in writing this blog is to openly reflect on how I’m staying healthy, being creative, and keeping busy during quarantine. Ideally, my fashion inspiration and lifestyle tips will empower you to be the best version of yourself that you could possibly be. Thank you guys so much for visiting my blog, and I hope that you’ll join me on this journey to becoming happier, healthier, and more empowered through 2020 and beyond!

Feel free to contact me via email or dm me on instagram if you would like to collaborate or even just chat. I’m super friendly and I love to meet new people, so don’t be afraid to reach out! :)
